▾ Bingo Boffin QuestLine

Go with Mr. Boffin on adventures and he will reward you with titles and items to barter with Bingo Badges . The quest line is beginner friendly and will follow along with you as you progress through the epic quest. There are a few quests that are a bit frustrating and obtuse — we will help you with those.

An Earlier Bingo Quest

Help Bingo gather supplies from the Shire before setting off on his journey. Speak to Milo Goodbody in Michel Delving to begin.

Bingo's Scattered Thoughts

Speak to dwarves for hints in the Twenty-First Hall and Flaming Deeps.

  • Defeat 2 Morovial Scouts in the Fanged Pit 3.5S, 103.3W
  • Defeat 3 White Hand Orcs near Orc-Watch, Malmezel 12.0S, 106.1W
  • Defeat 2 Warg Riders and 2 Moria Warriors near Orc-Watch, Budkhul-thârkh 11.8S, 107.0W
